Biography

I’m a postdoctoral visual neuroscientist at KU Leuven who’s interested in the computational and theoretical aspects of vision. Currently, I’m working with macaques in fMRI-guided electrophysiological studies to see how the brain processes and recognizes visual information from body movements, which is an important part of our nonverbal social communication and action recognition.

Interests

  • Predictive coding and Bayesian inference
